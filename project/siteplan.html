Anime World - Site Plan

By Hann Dowyne Valcourt

Site Name

Anime World - A guide to anime culture, series, and characters.

Reason for Name: The name “Anime World” reflects the idea of exploring anime as a vast, colorful universe filled with creativity and imagination.

Optional Domain: animeworldhub.net

Site Purpose

The purpose of Anime World is to introduce visitors to the world of anime. The site will provide guides, character spotlights, top series recommendations, and a random anime suggestion button. It will help both fans and newcomers discover and appreciate anime stories and art styles.

Scenarios

  • Which anime series should I start watching as a beginner?
  • Who are the most popular anime characters and what makes them unique?

Color Scheme

The color scheme is designed to give a soft anime vibe — combining pastel pinks and purples that represent energy, creativity, and serenity often seen in anime aesthetics.

Usage: Pink for accents, purple for headings, and white/light backgrounds for clean readability.

Typography

Font: Montserrat & Open Sans (Sans-serif)

  • Headings: Montserrat Bold — used for section titles and navigation to give a modern and confident look.
  • Body Text: Open Sans Regular — used for paragraphs and general text for clear readability.
  • Accent Text: Montserrat Medium — used for small highlights or buttons for visual emphasis.
